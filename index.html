Concert hall quality music for your special occasion

Having been a professional musician for forty five years, I feel confident in knowing what it takes to create a unique musical experience. I have carefully assembled the cream of the crop of finger style guitar arrangements, from Chet Atkins to Segovia, covering Bach to The Beatles, touching on every popular genre in between. As an added texture, I also play a set of Celtic tunes featuring the beautiful melodies of Ireland and Scotland.

Besides performing each piece flawlessly, my mission is to engage and interact with the audience. Using current events as well as history, my performance is anything but a classical guitar recital. The people laugh and actually have fun!
